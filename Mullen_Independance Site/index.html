Global Insight: Carbon Emissions by Region

One of the most significant indicators of global interdependence is the distribution of carbon emissions. While industrial nations produce the bulk of CO₂, developing regions are increasingly contributing as they industrialize. This simulated chart below visualizes estimated carbon output across major global regions, showing how interconnected our environmental and economic systems have become. Tackling climate change requires cooperation from all — no single nation can act alone.
